Our Story

Crafted for the
Gulf Coast

Gulf Coast Craftsmanship was built on a simple belief: every home deserves the same level of care and precision that goes into defending a country. We're a veteran-owned contracting company rooted in Southwest Florida, bringing military-grade discipline to every tile, floor, and renovation project we take on.

We don't cut corners. We don't disappear mid-project. We show up, we communicate, and we deliver work that stands the test of time — and the Florida climate.

  • Precision craftsmanship on every square foot
  • Transparent pricing — no surprise invoices
  • Direct communication from quote to completion
  • Work that carries our name long after we leave
  • Locally trusted, veteran-built, community-focused
What We Do

Three Service Lines.
One Standard of Excellence.

Tile & Stone Installation

Precision tile setting for floors, walls, showers, and outdoor spaces. We work with porcelain, natural stone, mosaic, and large-format tile — all set to stand up to Florida's humidity and heat.

  • Bathroom & shower tile
  • Kitchen backsplashes
  • Flooring & entryways
  • Pool decks & patios
  • Large-format & custom patterns

Flooring Solutions

From luxury vinyl plank to hardwood and engineered flooring, we install and refinish floors that look stunning from day one and hold up for decades in Southwest Florida's coastal environment.

  • Luxury vinyl plank (LVP)
  • Hardwood installation & refinishing
  • Engineered wood flooring
  • Laminate & waterproof options
  • Subfloor prep & leveling

Home Remodeling

Full-scope interior renovation from design consultation to final walkthrough. We coordinate every trade, manage the timeline, and deliver a finished space that exceeds what you envisioned.

  • Kitchen remodels
  • Bathroom renovations
  • Interior room transformations
  • Drywall, paint & trim work
  • Project management & coordination
